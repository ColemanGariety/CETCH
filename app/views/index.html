What is this?

CETCH is a computer science competition with participants from all over the world. Each week we solve a computer science puzzle of varying difficulty and upload our programs here. On Friday at midnight, all the entries are run in a sandbox and the execution times are clocked. The fastest solution wins. A new competition begins immediately after the previous one has ended.

Rules:

  1. You may only use languages as specified by the competition
  2. You may only use those language's standard libraries
  3. Your entry program may not contain the solution itself
